Heather "Sarafina" Hunt

I am

Bellydance

Heather began dancing at the age of 3 when she started ballet, tap and jazz. As an adult she began studying bellydance and has been performing ever since! Heather is an award winning bellydance artist who performs under the stage name “Sarafina”.

She is in high demand performing weekly at restaurants, hookah lounges and private events. Be sure to check her social media weekly to find out where she’ll be performing.

For an event that your guests will keep talking about, contact Heather to find out how she can make your special event even more special. Birthdays, anniversaries, grand openings, bachelorette parties, holidays and weddings are just a few of the possibilities. Any occasion can be made special with bellydance. Contact Heather to make your special event sparkle!

Heather also produces many public events throughout the year where you can come see many different performers, dancers and musicians, have a meal and do some shopping. Check her social media for more info.

Fitness

Heather is a NASM Certified Personal Trainer, NASM Fitness Nutrition Specialist and award winning bodybuilder.

She trains in person in Texas at the Plano Recreation Centers. She also trains all over virtually and in some cases can drive to your gym for training. She is a virtual coach and designs custom nutrition and workout programs.

Heather's clients range in age from 16 to 85, men and women. It's never too late to start working on a healthier you.

Contact Heather for all of your fitness needs. She can help you with fat loss, strength, lean muscle, mobility and so much more. Let’s get you on a path to your best self!

Cosplay

Heather first began attending comic cons as a teenager. In 2022 she made her cosplay guest debut at the Dallas Comic Show's Weekend of Cheer where she was also a judge for the costume contest. Look for her guest appearance at more conventions in 2023. Contact Heather to book her for your next cosplay event.

Model

Heather is a published model and has been modeling for several years. From bellydance to fitness to cosplay to fashion to artistic, Heather can do it all. Heather can do her own MUAH. Check out her social media for more samples of her work. Contact Heather for your next photoshoot.
